City administration begins ward-level cleanliness and water review drive

By City Desk • Updated today • 5 min read

The local administration has started a ward-level review drive focused on cleanliness, public water supply, complaint response and routine maintenance of public spaces.

The initiative is designed to make civic reporting easier for residents by publishing progress notes, verified complaint categories and basic ward-level status updates.

What residents should know

  • Weekly inspection summaries will be shared by the civic team.
  • Ward officers will collect public complaints through official channels.
  • Priority will be given to water leakage, street cleaning and drainage points.
